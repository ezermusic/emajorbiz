About E-Major Biznet

Discover Our Mission, Our Story and the People behind the content you Love.

Our Channel Story

E-Major Biznet was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between spiritual growth and practical life skills. We recognized that many believers were passionate about their faith but struggled to apply biblical principles to areas like financial management and entrepreneurship.

What started as a small YouTube channel with weekly Bible teachings has now grown into a comprehensive platform offering resources across multiple categories, including Gospel Music, Biblical Breakdowns, Financial Literacy, Business Ideas, and more.

Our community continues to grow as we remain committed to providing value-based content that inspires, educates, and empowers individuals to excel both spiritually and practically.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with biblical wisdom and practical resources that foster spiritual growth, financial literacy, and entrepreneurial success.

Our Vision

To be a global platform where faith meets practical living, creating a community of believers who are spiritually grounded and financially empowered.
