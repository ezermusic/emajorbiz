Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you upload new content?

We strive to upload new content at least twice a week across our different channels. Gospel music videos are typically released on Sundays, while financial literacy and business ideas content is uploaded on Wednesdays. Biblical breakdowns and teachings are published as they are completed, typically 1-2 times per month.

Can I suggest a song for your next biblical breakdown?

Absolutely! We love hearing song suggestions from our community. You can submit your request through our contact form or on our social media channels. While we can't guarantee that every suggestion will be featured, we carefully consider all recommendations.

Are all your resources free to download?

We offer a combination of free, donation-based, and premium resources. Many of our basic resources are completely free to download. For more comprehensive guides and tools, we offer them on a donation basis (pay what you can) or as premium resources with a fixed price. This helps us continue creating quality content while keeping most materials accessible to everyone.

How can I support your ministry?

There are several ways you can support our ministry:

  • Subscribe to our YouTube channel and share our content
  • Purchase our premium resources
  • Make a donation through our website
  • Become a monthly partner
  • Pray for our ministry and team

Every form of support, whether financial or non-financial, helps us continue our mission.

Do you offer speaking engagements or workshops?

Yes, we are available for speaking engagements, workshops, and events related to gospel music, biblical teachings, financial literacy, and business development. Please contact us through the form on this page with details about your event, and we'll get back to you to discuss availability and arrangements.

I'm having trouble downloading a resource. What should I do?

If you're experiencing issues with downloading resources, please try:

  1. Refreshing your browser
  2. Clearing your browser cache
  3. Using a different browser

If the problem persists, please contact our support team at support@emajorbiznet.com with details about the issue, including the resource you're trying to download and the device/browser you're using.
