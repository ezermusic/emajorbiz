Financial Stewardship in Modern Christianity: Balancing Faith and Financial Wisdom

April 20, 2025 45 minutes Episode #42

In this enlightening episode, we explore the biblical principles of financial stewardship and how they apply to modern Christians. We discuss practical strategies for managing personal finances while honoring faith-based values, and examine how proper financial management can support ministry and community work.

Our special guest, financial advisor Sarah Johnson, shares her expertise on creating faith-based financial plans and how to align investment choices with Christian principles.
